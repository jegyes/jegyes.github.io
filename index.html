Join us for an upcoming Discussion!

FreeRequisITes is a "Round-Table" reading and discussion group for lovers of "The Liberal Arts."

Discussion groups are open to all members. No reservation is required; however, we're happy to hear from you if you have questions or you'd like to chat before the meeting. While Discussion attendance is not required, and it is perfectly fine to pick and choose based on your topical interests, please note that some discussions build upon previous sessions and it may be helpful to check out the "Discussion Notes and Summaries" on the FreeRequisITes Forum.
